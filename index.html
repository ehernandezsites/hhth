Heidi Helping the Homeless

Myself and a few volunteers have been helping the homeless in and around Chickasha, Oklahoma since 2018. With the help of your donations, I can provide food and clothing for the homeless. In the future, I would also like to help with job and housing applications, social security and food stamp applications, and birth certificates. At the 98th annual Chickasha Chamber banquet, I was awarded Citizen of the Year.

I try to feed daily hot food and try to feed the homeless wherever they are. I have held multiple coat drives and have given over 500 coats away at no cost to the community. I also run blessing boxes where we can collect and distribute donations to the needy. Eventually we also want to work alongside local churches and soup kitchens.
