Heidi Helping the Homeless

Myself and a few volunteers have been helping the homeless in and around Chickasha, Oklahoma since 2018. With the help of your donations, I can provide food and clothing for the homeless. We also help with job and housing applications, social security and food stamp applications, and birth certificates. This project is funded entirely through community donations; read on to find out how you can help!



What We Need

  • •A building for our organization
